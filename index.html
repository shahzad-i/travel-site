Introduction of Gilgit-Baltistan

Gilgit-Baltistan (GB) is a breathtakingly beautiful region located in the northernmost part of Pakistan. Known as the "roof of the world," it is home to some of the highest mountain ranges on Earth, including the Karakoram, Himalayas, and Hindukush. Covering an area of approximately 72,000 square kilometers, Gilgit-Baltistan shares borders with China, Afghanistan, and India.
