Things to Do in Gilgit-Baltistan

  1. Trekking & Hiking
    2. Explore world-famous trails like: Fairy Meadows & Nanga Parbat Base Camp Rakaposhi Base Camp Passu Glacier Trek
  2. Camping in Valleys
    3. Set up a tent in serene locations like Hunza, Deosai, or Phander Valley and enjoy starry nights.
  3. Boating & Fishing
    4. Enjoy boating in Attabad Lake, Satpara Lake, or Upper Kachura Lake.
  4. Photography
    5. Capture the beauty of snow-covered peaks, colorful autumn trees, and historic forts.
  5. Explore Cultural Heritage
    6. Visit ancient forts like Baltit Fort, Shigar Fort, and Altit Fort. Experience local festivals and traditions.
  6. Winter Sports
    7. Try skiing and snowboarding in Naltar Ski Resort or explore snow valleys during winter.
  7. Taste Local Cuisine
    8. Eat traditional foods like: Chapshuro (Balti meat pie) Apricot juice and jams Buckwheat bread (Phitti)
  8. Visit Deosai National Park
    9. Discover the Land of Giants, with wildflowers, marmots, and panoramic landscapes.
  9. Walk Across Suspension Bridges Experience thrilling walks on:
    10. Hussaini Suspension Bridge Passu Suspension Bridge
  10. Shop Local Handicrafts
    11. Buy handmade wool shawls, caps, gems, dry fruits, and Balti embroidery.
